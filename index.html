Survey Result

These charts illustrate the result of a survey in which Santa Laurensia students were asked whether they are passive smokers or not, including the sex of the passive smokers, the grade the passive smokers at, the age of the passive smokers, the frequency of them inhaling smoke, and what they do when someone is smoking near them.

From 80 students participating in the survey, it is clear that most Santa Laurensia students do not become passive smokers, at 72.5%. The rest, at 22.5%, are passive smokers. The majority of the passive smokers are female, at 81.8%, and the rest are male. They are primarily seventh-graders, at 50%. Eighth grade has the second-highest students, at 36.4%. The grade with the minor students is senior high schoolers, at 13.6%. The majority age of the passive smokers in Santa Laurensia is 13 years, at 54.6%. The 12 years group represents 22.7% of the total. The 16-18 years group shares the third-highest number of students, at 13.6%. The age group with the minor students is 14 years, at 9.1%. The frequency of inhaling cigarette smoke by passive smokers is dominated by sometimes, at 45.5%. The seldom group has the second-highest percentage, at 31.8%, followed by often, at 18.2%. The smallest frequency group with the least student is always at 4.5%. Lastly, if the students are near a person smoking, the majority will avoid them, at 77.3%. The rest of them will ignore them and stay there.

These charts illustrate the result of a survey in which Santa Laurensia students were asked whether they are ever smoked or not, including sex of the smokers, the grade the smokers at, the age of the smokers, the type of cigarettes they smoked, the reason why they smoked, the number of cigarettes smoked in their entire life, and the last time they smoked.

From 80 students participating in the survey, it is clear that most Santa Laurensia students never smoked, at 96.3%. The rest, at 3.7%, are ever smoked. The majority of the smokers are male, two-thirds of the total and the rest are male, a third of the total. They are primarily senior high schoolers, two-thirds of the total. The grade with the minor students in seventh grade, a third of the total. The majority age of the passive smokers in Santa Laurensia is 16-18 years, two-thirds of the total. The age group with the most negligible student contribution is 12 years, one-third of the total. All the smokers have ever tried smoking using electronic cigarettes, but only two-thirds of the total ever tried smoking using tobacco cigarettes. Two-thirds of the smokers smoked because of curiosity, while the rest smoked because smoking is a fun activity to do with friends. The majority of the smokers, two-thirds of the total, smoked 1-10 cigarettes in their entire life. On the other hand, the other third smoked 11-100 cigarettes in his entire life. Two-thirds of the smokers last smoked over one year ago, while the other one-third last smoked within this month.

Analysis

From the analysis of the charts, Alam Sutera, the area in which most of the students live, is clean and free from smokers. This is because most of the survey participants do not passively smoke. It indicates that their environment is free from smokers and cigarettes. After that, they are primarily female because, in general, active smokers are male. Females and males are inseparable; thus, some females might be passive smokers. Next, most passive smokers are seventh graders because they are too young to smoke actively. Then, most seventh graders have older siblings, which probably smoke since they are mature enough. The majority of the passive smokers are 13 years old because the seventh graders are mostly 13 now. Besides that, the frequency of passive smokers inhaling the smoke is sometimes. This indicates that the place where passive smokers live contains a lot of active smokers. In addition, if someone is smoking near them, most of them will avoid that person. This shows that most of them are educated and know the effects of passive smoking.

In the other charts, most students are not active smokers, which means that they are well educated about the dangers of active smoking. Then, they are primarily male because smoking is a symbol of masculinity. After that, they are primarily senior high schoolers. This is because their parents already trust them and know that they understand the effects of smoking. Besides that, most smokers are 16-18 years old because they are mostly senior high schoolers. Furthermore, when smoking, they mostly use electronic cigarettes. This indicates that they are well educated and understand that electronic cigarettes are healthier than tobacco cigarettes. As well as that, the reason why they smoked is mostly curiosity. This may happen because they are imitating other people who smoke. Moreover, most of the number of cigarettes they consume in their entire life is 1-10 cigarettes. This further supports the reason why they smoked, which is curiosity. If they were serious about smoking, they would smoke more than 50 cigarettes in their entire life. Lastly, the majority of smokers last smoked over 12 months ago. This indicates that they may be curious about smoking or have already stopped smoking.

From the charts and analysis, it can be concluded that the place where the majority of the survey participants live, Alam Sutera, is free from smoking. In the place where the passive smokers live, there are quite a lot of active smokers there. The school and its environment have well-educated Santa Laurensia students. They can understand the effects of smoking, like causing diseases and addiction, as well as, trying either not to smoke or passively smoke. Besides that, they also know which type of cigarettes is better and healthier, which is electronic cigarettes.

Advice to Teens

It is recommended to stop and avoid smoking. Never try smoking. Once you try smoking, the nicotine in the cigarette will react and cause addiction. You will struggle to stop and remove that habit. If you smoke and try to stop, you can start reducing the number of cigarettes you consume every day. If you continue this every day, you can stop smoking entirely. If you are smoking tobacco cigarettes and you struggle to stop that, doctors recommend it to switch to electronic cigarettes. This is because electronic cigarettes are way healthier than tobacco cigarettes since they do not have any toxic substances.
